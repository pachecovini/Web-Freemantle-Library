Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone

Author: J.K. Rowling , Olly Moss (Illustrator)

Publication Year: First published June 26, 1997

Genre: Fantasy, Fiction, Young Adult, Magic, Childrens, Middle Grade

Synopsis: "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone" follows Harry, an orphaned boy who discovers he is a wizard on his 11th birthday. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes friends, learns magic, and uncovers secrets about his parents' deaths. Harry discovers the existence of the Sorcerer’s Stone and confronts the dark wizard Voldemort, who seeks to return to power.

Reviews

Reviewer 1: "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone ignited my imagination like no other book ever has. J.K. Rowling's enchanting tale of a young boy discovering his magical heritage swept me away into a world of wonder and adventure. From the moment Harry receives his letter from Hogwarts to his thrilling encounters with trolls and dark wizards, I was completely spellbound. The characters felt like old friends, and Hogwarts itself became a place I longed to visit. This book is a timeless classic that reminds us of the power of friendship, bravery, and the magic that exists within us all."

Reviewer 2: "Reading Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone was like discovering a hidden treasure trove of magic and mystery. J.K. Rowling's storytelling prowess shines brightly in this captivating tale of a boy who learns he is a wizard. From the bustling streets of Diagon Alley to the enchanted halls of Hogwarts, every page brims with imagination and excitement. Harry, Ron, and Hermione quickly became my heroes as they embarked on their quest to unravel the mysteries surrounding the Philosopher's Stone. This book is a delightful blend of humor, heart, and adventure that will enchant readers of all ages."

Reviewer 3: "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is a book that holds a special place in my heart. J.K. Rowling's imaginative world-building and richly drawn characters drew me in from the very first page. As Harry navigates the challenges of his new life at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, I found myself cheering him on every step of the way. From the quirky antics of the Weasley twins to the sinister plots of Voldemort, there's never a dull moment in this spellbinding story. This book is a magical journey that reminds us of the power of love, friendship, and the courage to stand up against darkness."
